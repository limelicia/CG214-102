The Chronicles of SWOOP

SWOOP is the product of the FUTURE, changing the way we travel.
Our founder and scientist, Linda Richards, had been working closely with scientists and engineers for the past 20 years, experimenting various anti-gravity possibilities. It was only in May 2016 that they had the breakthrough they had been waiting for...
We are very excited to share with you our new product (right), the SWOOP. The SWOOP is a shoe with anti-gravity properties. As defined by its name, SWOOP is a smart technology product that allows its wearer to manuever its speed of ascend and descend, sometimes mimicking the movement of a bird descending rapidly through the air.

The SWOOP Elements

    The SWOOP is:
  • shoe with a built-in anti-gravity technology
  • a solar chip attached to the design
  • waterproof
  • light-weight
  • made up of extremely breathable fabric and materials

The Power of SWOOP

    The SWOOP has:
  1. a solar chip that recharges from converting air pollutants to energy
  2. a cushioned lining that has the intelligence to analyse the joints of the feet to determine direction
  3. an outsole that vacuums the surrounding air, allowing wearer to hover on air
