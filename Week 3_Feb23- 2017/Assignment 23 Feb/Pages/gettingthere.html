Getting there

Wat Arun is easily accessible. It is located on the west bank of Chao Phraya River.

Visitors can consider the following options:

  1. Take the river boat from the Sapphan Taksin boat pier to pier 8. Thereafter, board the shuttle boat for only 3 baht.
  2. Take the river boat from Tha Tien Pier, near Wat Pho.
    • The Tha Tien express boat pier, at the southwest corner of the Grand Palace or Wat Phra Kaew, is diagonally opposite of Wat Arun, and boats ply at very frequent intervals.
    • You can get to Tha Tien on the Chao Phraya River Express boats from any other pier, or take a taxi to it.
    • Buses that go near Tha Tien are ordinary buses 1, 25, 44, 47, 62 and 91 that stop on Maharat road.

Admission

Entry to the temple is free for Thai citizens, but 30 baht for foreigners.

It is open daily from 8:30am to 5:30pm.
